About MSMV Catering

Our Story

Founded in the heart of Pune, MSMV Catering was born from a simple passion: to share the rich, authentic flavors of our heritage with our community. We believe that food is more than just sustenance; it's a celebration of culture, a way to bring people together, and a source of joy. Our journey began in a small kitchen, with family recipes passed down through generations, and has grown into a full-service catering company dedicated to creating unforgettable culinary experiences.

Our Philosophy

At MSMV, our philosophy is rooted in three core principles: authenticity, quality, and hospitality. We use only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients to ensure every dish is vibrant and flavorful. Our culinary team is committed to honoring traditional cooking methods while also embracing modern creativity. Above all, we strive to provide warm, impeccable service, ensuring that every event we cater is a seamless and memorable occasion for our clients and their guests.
